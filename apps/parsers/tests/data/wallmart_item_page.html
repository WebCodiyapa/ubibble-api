    Proctor Silex 12 Cup Programmable Coffeemaker:
    • Programmable clock
    • 12 cup capacity
    • 2 hour auto shutoff
    • 1-4 cup brewing option
    • Auto pause & serve
    • Dishwasher safe carafe & basket
    • Water window
    • 4 star consumer rated
    • Limited one year warranty
    This newly designed Proctor Silex 12-Cup Programmable Coffeemaker comes fully loaded with the features you are looking for. It has an automatic pause and serve function for use when coffee is still brewing. Its programming features allow you to wake up each morning to a freshly brewed cup of coffee. The Proctor Silex 12-cup coffeemaker comes with a two-hour automatic shut-off feature that prevents leaving it on while you're away. In addition, it has an easy-view water window that shows you how much water has been poured into the machine. Cleaning this unit is a breeze as the carafe and filter basket are dishwasher safe. You can use this Proctor Silex 12-Cup Programmable Coffeemaker to brew delicious hot coffee in just a few minutes, which is ideal when you have sudden visitors or are entertaining guests. You can also choose a 1- to 4-cup brewing option, depending on how many cups you want to make.

    The coffeemaker that I bought was the white version. Being white, it is easier to spot any areas that need to be cleaned. I find that the water fill compartment opening is somewhat small. The burner that the pot sits on works well and helps to make the coffee hot the way I like it. Timing is everything. The outlet on the left side is situated so that the top cannot be opened unless it is unplugged and moved forward which then requires re-programming the clock after re-positioning and re-connecting.
